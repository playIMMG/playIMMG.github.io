Mantis

Mantis is a fast-paced arcade game where the player defends the city by disarming bombs hurling down. Use your slice attack to destroy the bombs and recharge your double jump to maintain air time and rack up combos. Collect coins to upgrade your gear and abilities, and aim for higher and higher scores. The game is available on the Google Play Store, and it will soon be playable on this website here.
