Temple Ring

Temple Ring is an innovative 2D puzzle platformer where the player must choose the correct path and avoid obstacles to escape the dangerous maze of rings rolling and closing around you. Think fast and act quickly to solve the first two handcrafted levels, or choose the random maze to let the game create a unique maze around you for a fresh experience every run. Temple Ring is available now on the Google Play Store (link) and soon on this site here.

Janken Arena

Janken Arena is a top-down, fast-paced 3D hack-and-slash game where the player fights various monsters using special movement and attack powers that change according to the type of enemies in the arena. Like rock-paper-scissors, the player must attack the right type of enemies and avoid others. While controlling the fiery battle axe, hack down the electric-type robot enemies and be sure to avoid the water-type fish monsters. When controlling the ice bow, mark down the fire golems using your pool ability and snipe them down while avoiding the robots. With the lightning daggers, teleport and backstab the fish monsters, but be sure to avoid the golems. Defeat as many enemies as possible to rack up the highest score. Now available on the Google Play Store (link) and soon on this website here.
